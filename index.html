About Me

I'm an aspiring UX designer, getting a bachelor’s degree in THUAS.
My role is to mediate between people and emerging technologies
by providing the most relevant and desirable interaction experience.
Driven by curiosity and ardour, I strive to define problems
and bring solutions that make the interplay of a human
with a digital product seamless, effective and beneficial.

While I may not have extensive industry experience in design,
I have spent the past 2.5 years working in marketing and honing
my communication skills through social media.
During this time, I gained valuable insights into user behavior
and how to communicate with specific audiences.
Now, I am excited to apply this knowledge, learn more,
and share my enthusiasm with the world.
