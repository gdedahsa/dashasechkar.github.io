Case of Social Media Marketing

Seaside Bazaar

Located in Saint Petersburg, Seaside Bazaar was a one-of-a-kind public market where vendors sold a diverse range of products, including ceramics, plants, clothing, candles, and food.
For this project, I was responsible for creating a business account from scratch, including filling it with relevant content and necessary information for the user. To ensure a cohesive design, I created a grid layout one week prior to posting to maintain consistency and visual appeal across all posts.

Garage Sale in Port

I previously worked for Garage Sale in Port - a massive vintage market
that provides an opportunity for individuals to sell their unique clothing
and treasures.
With a diverse selection of products available, visitors can find everything fromfurniture to vintage sunglasses while enjoying the thrill of thrifting.
As part of this project, I developed a comprehensive strategy that included creating a content grid, designing graphics, selecting photos, and executing daily posts.
